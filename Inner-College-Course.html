MSRIT B.E in Aerospace Engineering Course Details:

B.E Aerospace Engineering at M.S Ramaiah Institute of Technology (MSRIT), Bangalore, is a 4-year undergraduate program that focuses on design, development, and maintenance of aircraft and spacecraft. The curriculum covers core topics such as mechanical, electrical, and computer science with aerospace-specific modules to provide students with a strong foundation in aerodynamics, propulsion, avionics, and structural analysis.

The program is structured to offer a blend of theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience through laboratory sessions, simulation-based learning, and industry internships. Through the course, MSRIT College aims to equip students with the technical expertise and analytical skills needed to excel in the aerospace and defense sectors.

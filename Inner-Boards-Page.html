About Vidya Bharath College of Pharmacy, Bangalore:

Vidya Bharath College of Pharmacy, Bangalore, is a private institution managed by the Shri Maruthi Group of Institutions. The college is dedicated to providing value-based academic programmes in a supportive environment to equip students with the necessary knowledge and skills that can help them grow in the pharmaceutical industry and other related healthcare fields. The college has a distinguished history of helping talented students sharpen their skills and implement the acquired knowledge across various career paths.

The faculty at Vidya Bharath College of Pharmacy, Bangalore, is well-qualified, young, and dynamic. The teacher-to-student ratio at the college is 1:10. This is done to ensure that each teacher addresses the academic and other basic needs of 10 students. Additionally, the college invites renowned scholars, respected dignitaries, and teachers who are well-versed in the education sector, specifically the pharmaceutical industry, to offer guest lectures, workshops, and seminars.
